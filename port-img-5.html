5/9 Brewery Beer Labels

Creating and designing custom beer labels was one of my favourite projects to work on. My client brews craft beer from his home and wanted custom beer labels for himself. I first created a logo for the client, incorporating the name of his “brewery” then elements that tied into his beer making. The X is created with two beer paddles (used to stir beer), there is a hop at the top and the Japanese symbol for gold at the bottom. The gold symbol is to represent the 5/9. 5/9 is the purest form of gold ever created at the Mint. After the logo was created we printed labels and placed them on the bottles. I then continued to create custom beer glasses, t shirts and coasters for the client.
