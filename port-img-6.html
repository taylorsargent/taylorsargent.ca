Bakers Bean Re-Branding

Bakers Bean is a small local coffee shop located in the heart of Richmond. This small coffee shop had a bright orange and black logo with all their packaging and interior following in suit. I chose to redesign the logo and packaging to create a more relaxing and modern atmosphere for this coffee shop. I redesigned the logo with a bold sans-serif font paired with a thin sans-serif. I wanted a simple clean logo that would stand out and make a point. I chose a blue and brown theme for their branding because the two colours work well together to create a clean, calming atmosphere.
