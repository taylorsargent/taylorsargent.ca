about me

I am twenty-one years old and a recent graduate of the graphic design program from Algonquin College. I am working full-time as a junior designer for a marketing agency in the west end of Ottawa. I am always looking and open to freelance projects! I am currently taking an online course through Algonquin College on social media marketing and am loving every second. When I am not working or studying I love to travel, read, grab coffee with friends and spend time with my two cats.
