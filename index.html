About Me

From the time I was little I was always creating art, whether it was with paint, crayons, sticks or anything I could get my hands on. As I grew older my love for creating also grew. I took my love for art and creating to a new level when I entered high school and was introduced to communication technology. Since that first class I knew that I wanted to become a graphic designer.

I am currently twenty one years old and in my third and final year of the Graphic Design program at Algonquin College. I am both nervous and excited at the same time to be graduating this April. I enjoy all areas of design but the ones I want to pursue the most are Packaging and Branding. Packaging design to me is the most interesting because you are able to design something and turn it into a physical item you can hold and use. I hope to one day go into a store and point to something on the shelf and say "That right there, I created that!".
