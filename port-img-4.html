Whittards Tea Packaging

Whittards Tea is a prestige tea store located in London, England. I redesigned their classic tea package to be more Eco-friendly and reduce waste. The package was a rectangle container with almost half of it empty space. I recreated the package into a hexagon box that is easily stackable for shelf space. The container has all the information on it and uses the original logo. The colours used on the package come from the original logo. I chose to use a sans-serif font because it is legible at small sizes and helps bring in a more modern side to the company. The die lines above show the top and bottom of the package as well as the insert for the inside of the lid and a band that wraps around the container.
