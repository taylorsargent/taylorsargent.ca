Yoga Basics Brochure

Typography was never my strong suit but when it came to redesigning this yoga brochure I was all over it. I changed the colours in the logo and went with a deep blue and orange to complement each other. I chose a sans-serif font to keep it clean and legible. This brochure had a lot of content for a small spread. I used rules and colour blocks to separate some of the content. I used a three column grid to give the brochure a better visual layout and keep things clean and in order. I chose to put all pull-quotes in a different colour font to help them stand out against all the over content.
