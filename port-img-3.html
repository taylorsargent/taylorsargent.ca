Pretty Pots Re-Branding

Pretty Pots Flower Shop was looking to change their brand to suit a more modern and younger audience. This flower shop has been located in Stittsville for many years and has taken on the reputation of being “old” and “a shop for your parents”. I recreated a logo for them using a simple illustration and sans-serif fonts to appeal to a younger crowd and create a more modern sleek look and feel. I then created a branding guideline book for them to follow. I created stationary, envelopes and business cards that when together they form the cherry blossom flower from the logo. This logo can be used for all sort of flower shop paraphernalia, such as flower wrapping paper, paper bags, tissue paper, sign-age and even mugs.
